# Validation & Readiness

## Prerequisites

| SDLC | DSLC | Data Product Lifecycle | MLOps Lifecycle | LLMOps Lifecycle |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Application functions implemented and internally tested | Candidate analytical models available for evaluation | Gold layer and business metrics available | Candidate models trained and benchmarked | Functional RAG / GenAI Prototype |

## Steps

| SDLC | DSLC | Data Product Lifecycle | MLOps Lifecycle | LLMOps Lifecycle |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Is the application ready for production? | Are the analytical conclusions trustworthy and accepted by stakeholders? | Is the data product governed, certified, and consumable? | Is the model accurate, fair, explainable, and approved? | Is the AI solution safe, grounded, and production-ready? |
| User Acceptance Testing | Analytical results validation | Data quality certification | Accuracy validation | Prompt engineering |
| Performance testing | Statistical significance assessment | Governance approval | Precision/Recall evaluation | Retrieval evaluation |
| Security validation | Business hypothesis validation | Lineage verification | Bias testing | Groundedness testing |
| Release readiness review | Analytical model review | Access certification | Explainability review | Hallucination testing |
| Operational readiness | Stakeholder review and acceptance | Product approval | Model approval board | Guardrail validation |
| Defect remediation validation | Experiment reproducibility validation | Data contract validation | Feature validation | Prompt version validation |
| Documentation review | Methodology review | Metadata certification | Model documentation review | Knowledge source certification |
| Production go-live approval | Business sign-off | Data steward sign-off | Model governance sign-off | Responsible AI sign-off |
